MYVC Management System

The Montréal Youth Volleyball Club is dedicated to developing, promoting, and enhancing youth volleyball in various communities. Our mission is to guide young athletes aged 11 to 18 years toward becoming professional volleyball players by providing them with structured training programs, competitive opportunities, and a supportive environment

This web-based system empowers club administrators, coaches, and family members to efficiently manage the club’s operations. Key features include:

location_on
Location Management: Maintain and oversee branch and head locations.
groups
Personnel Management: Assign roles and track mandates for staff.
person_add
Member Registration: Add and manage club members with real-time status updates.
sports_volleyball
Team Formation: Create team sessions, assign roles, and manage schedules.
credit_card
Payment Management: Monitor membership payments and ensure active status.
insights
Reports & Analytics: Generate detailed reports to track performance and activities.
notifications_active
Automated Notifications: Ensure seamless communication with members.
