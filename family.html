Family Management

The Family Management page allows administrators to oversee and manage family member information efficiently. Each family member can be categorized as a Primary or Secondary contact and is associated with one or more club members. Detailed information is maintained, including first name, last name, SIN, Medicare card number, phone number, address, city, province, postal code, and the relationship with the associated club member(s).
