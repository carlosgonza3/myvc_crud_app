Club Member Management

The Club Member Management page allows administrators to efficiently manage and track information related to club members. Each club member is associated with a registered family member and can only be enrolled if they are between 11 and 18 years old. Detailed information includes the club membership number, first name, last name, date of birth, height, weight, SIN, Medicare card number, phone number, address, city, province, postal code, and the relationship with their associated family member.
