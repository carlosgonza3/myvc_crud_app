Personnel Management

The Personnel Management page allows administrators to manage and track the roles and mandates of all personnel efficiently. Each personnel member is assigned one or more roles, such as Administrator, Coach, Captain, or other positions, with detailed information such as first name, last name, SIN, Medicare card number, telephone number, address, email address, and mandate type (volunteer or salaried). This page ensures that the system keeps accurate records of all personnel operating at one or multiple locations.
